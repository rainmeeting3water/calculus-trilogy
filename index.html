定積分definite integral 是累積量 accumulation，不定積分indefinite integral 是反導函數族 family of anti-derivatives，而 微積分基本定理Fundamental Theorem of Calculus 把兩者真正接起來。Definite integrals represent accumulation; indefinite integrals represent families of anti-derivatives; the Fundamental Theorem of Calculus connects the two.

學習路徑Learning Pathway

第一站Stage 1

定積分：累積量與黎曼和Definite Integrals: Accumulation and Riemann Sums

從很多小矩形開始，理解 ∫ab f(x) dx 代表從 a 到 b 的帶符號累積量。A definite integral represents signed accumulation over an interval from a to b.

核心重點Key Ideas
  • 小矩形近似rectangle approximation
  • 有向貢獻signed contribution
  • 黎曼和Riemann sum
  • 帶符號面積signed area

不定積分：從微分倒著走Indefinite Integrals: Anti-differentiation and Families of Curves

從 F'(x)=f(x) 反推 F(x)，理解 ∫f(x)dx=F(x)+C 是一整族反導函數。An indefinite integral represents a family of anti-derivatives, often shown as a family of curves.

核心重點Key Ideas
  • 反導函數anti-derivative
  • 函數族family of functions / family of curves
  • 常數 Cconstant C
  • 微分檢查verify by differentiation

第三站Stage 3

微積分基本定理：把兩者接起來The Fundamental Theorem of Calculus: Connecting Accumulation and Anti-derivatives

觀察面積函數 A(x)=∫ax f(t)dt 的變化率，理解為什麼 A'(x)=f(x)。The accumulation function has derivative f(x).

核心重點Key Ideas

  • 平均高度average height
  • 瞬間變化率instantaneous rate of change

教師使用建議Teaching Suggestions

  1. 若學生尚未理解累積量，建議先從「定積分模組」開始。If students are new to accumulation, begin with the Definite Integral module.
  2. 若學生已熟悉微分，可以接著進入「不定積分模組」，理解反導函數族。If students already know differentiation, move on to anti-derivatives and families of curves.
  3. 最後進入「微積分基本定理模組」，把累積量與反導函數連接起來。Finally, use the Fundamental Theorem of Calculus to connect accumulation and anti-derivatives.
  4. 三個模組可分三節課使用，也可作為一個完整的探究式學習活動。The three modules can be taught across three lessons or as one complete inquiry activity.
