STEP 1｜從微分倒著想Think backward from differentiation

學生任務 Student Task

觀察 f(x)，思考哪一個 F(x) 微分後會得到它。

教師提問 Teacher Prompt

If F'(x)=f(x), what could F(x) be?

觀察重點 Key Observation

Indefinite integral is connected to anti-differentiation.

STEP 2｜理解反導函數Understand anti-derivatives

學生任務 Student Task

比較 f(x) 與 F(x) 的關係。

教師提問 Teacher Prompt

How can we verify that F(x) is an anti-derivative of f(x)?

觀察重點 Key Observation

只要 F'(x)=f(x)，F(x) 就是 f(x) 的 anti-derivative。

STEP 3｜觀察常數 CObserve the constant C

學生任務 Student Task

調整 C，觀察圖形如何上下平移。

教師提問 Teacher Prompt

Why does changing C not change the derivative?

觀察重點 Key Observation

The constant C shifts the graph vertically, but the derivative remains the same.

STEP 4｜理解函數族Understand a family of curves

學生任務 Student Task

比較不同 C 值形成的圖形。

教師提問 Teacher Prompt

Why does an indefinite integral represent a family of curves?

觀察重點 Key Observation

An indefinite integral represents a family of anti-derivatives.

STEP 5｜連到符號 ∫f(x)dx=F(x)+CConnect to the notation ∫f(x)dx=F(x)+C

學生任務 Student Task

用自己的話說明 +C 的意義。

教師提問 Teacher Prompt

Why must we include +C?

觀察重點 Key Observation

+C records all possible anti-derivatives.
